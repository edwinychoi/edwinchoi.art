I'm Edwin, and this is a space to share my visual wanderings for quiet presence. Reach out if you'd like to say hi, share work, or expand each other's way of creatively experiencing the world. edwinchoi.design@gmail.com

I want to capture the impermanence of charcoal powder resting on paper, lines that could disappear, light washes that blend. Napping in the park with others. Blue hour where the city and light blur into each other. Stepping into untouched morning snow. First coffee of the day. A place for introspection, if you want it. Or a place to simply be, if you don't.

By day, I design healthcare products to improve people's well-being. By night, I study public health informatics to use the design process to shape better healthcare systems. I needed a place inbetween. Here, I draw, so that I can have an unfinished (by design) playground of evolving visual experiments, following the pursuit of quiet presence wherever it goes.
